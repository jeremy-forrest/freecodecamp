Creamy Scrambled Eggs

This recipe will guide you through making creamy scrambled eggs.

Ingredients

  • 4 large eggs
  • 1/4 cup milk or cream
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 1 tablespoon butter
  • Optional: chopped herbs, cheese, or vegetables

Instructions

  1. Crack the eggs into a bowl.
  2. Add the milk or cream, salt, and pepper. Whisk until well combined.
  3. Heat a non-stick skillet over medium-low heat and add the butter.
  4. Once the butter is melted and slightly bubbly, pour in the egg mixture.
  5. Let the eggs sit undisturbed for a few seconds until they start to set around the edges.
  6. Using a spatula, gently stir the eggs, pushing them from the edges to the center.
  7. Continue to cook, stirring occasionally, until the eggs are mostly set but still slightly runny.
  8. Remove from heat; the residual heat will finish cooking the eggs.
  9. Serve immediately, garnished with optional herbs, cheese, or vegetables if desired.
