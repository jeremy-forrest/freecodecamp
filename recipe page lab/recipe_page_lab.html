Creamy Scrambled Egg Burrito

Just like Gordon's eggs from the video, but in a wrap!

Ingredients

  • 2 Eggs
  • Butter
  • Sour Cream
  • Large Tortilla

Instructions

  1. Mix in the eggs (unscrambled) with a tablespoon of butter.
  2. Keep the heat low!
  3. Keep stirring!
  4. Stir in a tablespoon of sour cream at the end.
  5. Wrap it all up in a tortilla, add tobasco or salsa.
